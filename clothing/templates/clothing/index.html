Welcome to My Little Shop!
This application allows you (Vendor) to manage your products, partners and sales.
  1. "Price List" tab shows what products you have available for production and their price.
  2. "My Storage" tab shows products and how many of them you have in stock.
  3. "Partners" tab shows your partners and their info.
  4. "Partners' Storages" tab shows your partners and products that you sent to them and how many.
  5. "Sales" tab shows confirmed sales of the products that you sent to your partners.

If you created a superuser, you can change all of the records via Django admin site.
